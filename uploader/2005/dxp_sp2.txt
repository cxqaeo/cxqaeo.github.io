 Altium Nexar 2004

 Nexar is the electronics design industry’s first comprehensive, vendor-
 independent system for designing embedded systems on an FPGA platform.

 Unlike other tools on the market, Nexar does not require engineers to have
 or acquire HDL/RTL experience in order to put systems onto an FPGA. Instead
 it uses familiar (board-level) design methodologies to implement complete
 system-level applications within programmable chips, making the potential of
 FPGA-based embedded systems design accessible to mainstream engineers. 

 Altium Protel 2004

 Protel is a complete board-level design solution that provides all of the
 capabilities needed to take any board-level design project from concept to
 completion.

 The latest release, Protel 2004, introduces support for the design of FPGAs,
 and automated FPGA pin optimization and pin synchronization between PCB and
 FPGA projects.

 Protel 2004 features hierarchical, multi-channel schematic capture for both
 PCB and FPGA implementation, mixed-signal simulation, and signal integrity
 analysis, through to rules-driven board layout, Situs Topological
 autorouting and CAM file checking and editing.

 Altium CircuitStudio 2004

 CircuitStudio 2004 is a universal front-end engineering system for both board
 -level and programmable logic design. 

 CircuitStudio is for engineers who want a fully-featured capture environment
 that addresses both PCB and FPGA design requirements. It combines hierarchical
 schematic capture, mixed-signal circuit simulation, VHDL design entry and
 simulation, pre-layout signal integrity analysis and an extensive range of
 integrated component libraries.

 Based on Altium's LiveDesign-enabled DXP technology integration platform,
 CircuitStudio is the perfect environment for front-end engineers collaborating
 with designers using Altium's Protel 2004 or Nexar 2004 design systems.

 Altium CAMtastic 2004

 CAMtastic 2004 bridges the gap between PCB design and manufacturing, allowing
 both board designers and fabrication engineers to quickly and easily verify
 critical manufacturing information.

 CAMtastic 2004 comes with a complete range of import/export options, check/fix
 analyses, and graphical and geometrical tools. Advanced features such as
 bi-directional ODB++ support and multi-step panelization ease the process of
 taking design data from layout through to fabrication.
